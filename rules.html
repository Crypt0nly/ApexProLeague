Official Rulebook

Binding for all registered clubs. By registering on Discord, your club agrees to every rule on this page. Last updated 11 June 2026.

01 Eligibility & Registration

  • Registration happens exclusively through the official Discord server via a ticket in #registration. No other channel is valid.
  • Each club must register a club name, club tag (3 letters), platform(s), a captain and a vice-captain with reachable Discord handles.
  • All players must be members of the league Discord server for the duration of the season.
  • Players may be registered with one club only. Playing for a second club results in immediate suspension.
  • The league is cross-play: PS5, Xbox Series X|S and PC clubs compete together.
  • Slots are limited to 16 clubs, verified on a first-come, first-served basis. Verified reserve clubs join a waitlist.
Captains: you are your club's single point of contact. Admin instructions delivered to the captain count as delivered to the whole club.

02 Rosters & Transfers

  • Rosters contain a minimum of 11 and a maximum of 15 players, locked at the group draw.
  • A club must field at least 8 human players in every official match. The remaining slots may be AI.
  • One transfer window opens between the group stage and the quarter-finals: up to 2 roster changes, subject to admin verification. Players who already appeared for another club in this season are cup-tied.
  • Gamertag changes must be reported to an admin before the player's next match.
  • Account sharing, ringers and smurfing are strictly prohibited and treated as match-fixing (see §8).

04 Scheduling & Forfeits

  • Default kick-off is 20:00 UK / 21:00 CET on the published matchday.
  • Captains may mutually reschedule within ±2 days of the matchday. Both captains must confirm the new time in their match thread on Discord.
  • A 15-minute grace period applies from the agreed kick-off. A club unable to field 8 human players after the grace period forfeits 0–3.
  • Two forfeits in a season result in removal from the competition; remaining fixtures are voided or awarded per admin decision.
  • Knockout matches cannot be rescheduled except by admin decision in exceptional circumstances.

05 Disconnects & Lag

  • If the match disconnects before the 20th in-game minute at 0–0, replay from kick-off in full.
  • If it disconnects later, the host recreates the lobby; the remaining time is played out and scores are aggregated across both sessions.
  • Persistent severe lag: capture video evidence, finish the half, then raise a ticket. Admins may order a replay on a neutral host.
  • Intentional disconnecting to avoid a result is treated as a forfeit plus a conduct penalty.
Tip: record your gameplay (console clips are fine). Most disputes are resolved in minutes when footage exists.

06 Results & Evidence

  • The winning captain (or home captain after a draw) posts the result in #match-results within 1 hour of full time.
  • Required evidence: a screenshot of the full-time match facts screen showing both club names and the score.
  • Player goals, assists, clean sheets and match ratings are submitted in the same post for the leaderboards.
  • Results are official once an admin reacts with the verification mark; tables and the site update at that point.

07 Conduct & Fair Play

  • Zero tolerance for hate speech, harassment, or threats — in-game, on Discord, or on streams. This is a removal-level offence.
  • No exploiting game-breaking bugs, glitched tactics or kick-glitches. Admins maintain a current exploit list in #rulebook.
  • Match-fixing, win-trading and betting on league matches by participants are removal-level offences.
  • Excessive time-wasting designed to abuse the disconnect rules is a conduct offence.
  • Treat casters, admins and opponents with respect. Banter is football; abuse is not.

08 Disputes & Penalties

  • Disputes are raised by ticket within 12 hours of full time. Include all evidence at submission.
  • A neutral panel of at least two admins rules within 24 hours. Panel decisions are final.
  • Penalty ladder: warning → goal/point deduction → match forfeit → player suspension → club removal. Severity sets the entry point — match-fixing and hate speech enter at removal.
  • Penalties for individuals can extend across seasons.

09 Prizes

  • Prize pool: €2,500 — €1,400 champions, €700 runners-up, €400 third place.
  • Individual awards: Golden Boot, Golden Glove, Playmaker, Season MVP.
  • Prizes are paid to the registered club captain within 7 days of the Grand Final; distribution within the club is the captain's responsibility.
  • Clubs removed for conduct or integrity offences forfeit all prize claims.

10 Admin Authority

League admins may rule on any situation not covered by this rulebook, and may amend the rulebook between matchdays with notice in #announcements. The spirit of the rules — fair, competitive, respectful football — outweighs any attempt to exploit their letter.

Questions about a rule? Open a ticket on Discord before kick-off, not after the final whistle.
