FAQ

Questions, answered

How do I register my club?

Join our Discord server and open a ticket in the #registration channel. You'll need a club name, platform, captain contact and a roster of 11–15 players. Admins verify entries within 48 hours.

Is there an entry fee?

No. Season I is completely free to enter. The prize pool is funded by the league and its partners.

Which platforms can compete?

The league runs on cross-play, so clubs on PS5, Xbox Series X|S and PC all compete in one bracket. Your whole roster doesn't need to be on the same platform.

When are matches played?

Group stage matchdays are Tuesdays and Fridays at 20:00 UK / 21:00 CET. Captains can mutually agree to reschedule within the same matchday window — see the rulebook for details.

What happens if a team doesn't show up?

A 15-minute grace period applies. After that the match is forfeited 3–0. Two forfeits result in removal from the competition. Full details are in the official rulebook.

How many players do I need?

Matches are 11v11 Pro Clubs. You must field at least 8 human players per match; rosters are capped at 15 with mid-season transfer rules in the rulebook.

Will matches be streamed?

Featured group games, all knockout rounds and the Grand Final are broadcast with casters on the league's channels. Clubs are free to stream their own POV.
